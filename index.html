About

I'm a 12th-grade student at Dhaka College, pursuing science with a focus on mathematics and physics. My academic roots go back to Ideal School and College, where I completed my SSC.

Two questions pull at me equally: how intelligence can emerge from computation, and why the universe behaves the way it does. That tension drives most of what I study and build.

Outside of competition work, I write code — ML pipelines, Discord bots, hardware experiments with microcontrollers. I think the best way to understand something is to build a broken version of it first.
